A closer look

Features

Everything for a good night

Simple, warm, and unhurried — the way bedtime should feel.

A Shelf for the Night

Fifty-three timeless tales — Austen, Verne, Grimm, and friends — arranged on beautiful nighttime shelves, with your Continue row waiting right on top.

Conjure a Story

Pick a place, a mood, and a length — Nightshelf writes a calm original bedtime story just for tonight, complete with its own cover.

Read Along, or Listen

Six warm narrators read every story aloud while the text follows along. Voice, pace, and reading mode — all yours to set.

Ambient Sound & Sleep Timer

Rain, fireplace, ocean — layered gently under the narration, with a sleep timer that fades everything out as you drift off.

Private by Design

No account, no sign-in, no ads, no trackers. Your shelf and your progress live on your device — Nightshelf keeps no copy on any server.

Right Where You Left Off

Your place in every story is held overnight and synced privately through iCloud, so tomorrow's chapter starts exactly where tonight's ended.

The ritual

A little ritual, every night

Nightshelf is built around the quiet minutes before sleep.

2

3

Drift off

The sleep timer fades the story out and holds your place till tomorrow.
