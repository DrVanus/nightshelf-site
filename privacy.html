Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Nightshelf ("the app", "we", "us") is a reading companion for iOS. Your reading life is personal, and we treat it that way. This policy explains what the app collects and why.

Information we collect

  • Your library & reading activity — the books on your shelves, your progress, notes, and streaks. This is stored to run the app and, where you enable it, synced privately across your devices.
  • Preferences — the genres and moods you browse, used to tailor recommendations to your taste.
  • Diagnostics — anonymized crash and performance data to help us fix bugs. It contains no book titles or personal identifiers.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, or browsing history outside the app.

How we use information

  • To keep your library, progress, and notes available across your devices.
  • To generate personalized recommendations based on what you read.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the app.

Third-party services

We use Apple services for the App Store and, where enabled, iCloud sync. Book metadata and cover images may be retrieved from catalog providers. These providers process data only to deliver their function and under their own terms.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data, and we do not share your reading activity with advertisers.

Children's privacy

Nightshelf is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Your choices

You can use Nightshelf entirely on-device, clear your library at any time, or request deletion of any synced data by contacting us.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hi@nightshelf.app.
